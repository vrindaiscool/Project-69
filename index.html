Campus Life

School Church

Established in 1957

Our Church is a place of welcoming and and love. Father Henry is our priest and teaches us to love one another as Jesus had said to the didciples.

School Hall

Established in 1957

Our School Hall is mainly used for assemblys! It is also sometimes used for liturgy's! You can also book the hall for a specfiic time for special events such as birthdays, performances etc. It is also a place where we gather as a whole school :)

School Library

Established in 1957

Our School Library is wonderful place to borrow and read books. It is also used by our wonderful reading and recovery teachers, to help students with comprehenshion and readings skills! Not only that but we also use our library for fun activities such as Book Fair and many more!
